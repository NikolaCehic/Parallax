# Code Of Conduct

Parallax follows a simple standard: be direct, respectful, and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Assume good intent.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Keep discussion focused on reproducible behavior and user impact.
- Be especially careful around financial claims, safety boundaries, and user trust.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Publishing private data or credentials.
- Encouraging unsafe financial behavior through the project.
- Repeatedly ignoring maintainer guidance on safety or scope.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributions that violate this code.
