# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Parallax is for individual analysts, active investors, portfolio teams, quant/research operators, and compliance or model-risk reviewers. They use it when a market idea needs disciplined review, evidence checks, invalidators, lifecycle monitoring, and an audit trail.

## Product Purpose

Parallax turns a trading thesis into an evidence-linked dossier. It should help users think more clearly, document the reasoning path, challenge assumptions, surface vetoes, and monitor whether the thesis remains valid. Success means unsupported conviction becomes hard to hide, and users can explain why a thesis is blocked, watchlisted, or only suitable for paper testing.

## Brand Personality

Calm, exacting, accountable. The product should feel like a serious research desk with model-risk discipline, not a signal room, broker terminal, or hype dashboard.

## Anti-references

- AI stock pickers that imply certainty or guaranteed alpha.
- Crypto-terminal neon, casino-like urgency, and gamified trading prompts.
- Marketing-heavy SaaS dashboards with oversized hero copy in the product shell.
- Decorative finance themes such as navy/gold prestige styling or chart wallpaper.
- Interfaces that hide risk language behind confident generated prose.

## Design Principles

- Evidence first: the UI should show what passed, what failed, and what is missing before it celebrates any result.
- Research, not execution: product copy and affordances must avoid live-order framing in general workflows.
- Make gates visible: readiness, provider, data, identity, storage, and model controls should be inspectable at a glance.
- Repair with restraint: setup flows should explain and apply local-safe fixes without hiding policy or secret boundaries.
- Onboard without shared secrets: invite flows should show one-time tokens clearly while proving persisted state keeps only hashes and scoped identity records.
- Separate admin and tenant surfaces: non-admin consoles should fetch only tenant-scoped/account data and avoid control-plane visibility by default.
- Dense but calm: analysts need enough information to scan, compare, and act without visual noise.
- Auditability over charm: every generated answer should point back to artifacts, dossier records, or validation evidence.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA for product surfaces. Use high-contrast text, visible focus states, semantic structure, keyboard-reachable controls, reduced-motion-friendly transitions, and status indicators that do not rely on color alone.
